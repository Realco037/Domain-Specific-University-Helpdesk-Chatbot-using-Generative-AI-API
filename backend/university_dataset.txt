ATTENDANCE:
Minimum 75% attendance is required in each course.
Students below 75% attendance are not allowed to sit for exams.
Students with very low attendance (below ~65%) may be detained or fail.

EXAM RULES:
Students must carry ID card and admit card during exams.
End-Term Exams are conducted at the end of each semester.
If a student fails a course, they must reappear by paying a fee.

CLASSES:
Students are required to attend all scheduled classes including lectures, tutorials, and practical sessions.
Attendance is recorded for each class and contributes to overall course attendance.
Missing classes without valid reason may reduce attendance percentage.
Students are expected to be punctual and attend classes regularly.
Class schedules are provided at the beginning of the semester through the university portal or timetable.
Make-up classes may be conducted in case of missed sessions or syllabus completion.

EVALUATION SYSTEM:
Evaluation includes Continuous Assessment (20-30%) and End-Term Exams (70-80%).
Students can apply for re-evaluation by paying a fee.

ACADEMIC RULES:
Students must follow the academic calendar and course structure.
Exam schedules are announced via university portals or notice boards.

GENERAL RULES:
Students must follow university code of conduct and discipline policies.
ID cards must be carried at all times inside campus.

DRESS CODE:
Students must wear formal attire during classes and exams.
Lab coats are required during practical sessions in labs.

HOSTEL RULES:
Hostel facilities are provided on yearly basis.
Students must follow hostel rules and restrictions.
Certain electrical appliances are not allowed in hostels.

ADDITIONAL:
Students must participate in academic and co-curricular activities.
University policies may change as per official guidelines.
